40.7 ly | 1 star | 7 planets
Celestia addons for the "seven spheres" themselves, the planets orbiting the ultracool dwarf star TRAPPIST-1. All of them are similar in size and mass to Earth, and three or four orbit within the habitable zone. The system is a target for atmospheric spectroscopy with the James Webb Space Telescope.

TRAPPIST-1 (New)

This addon has textures updated based on JWST results, currently only for the two innermost planets. JWST observations suggest that TRAPPIST-1 b lacks a significant atmosphere and has a low albedo, and that TRAPPIST-1 c similarly lacks a thick Venus-like atmosphere and may have a thin atmosphere or none.
Authors: SevenSpheres
